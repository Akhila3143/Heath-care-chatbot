Frequently Asked Questions

What services does the healthcare chatbot provide?
Our healthcare chatbot provides personalized health tips, food recommendations, and medicine suggestions based on your individual health needs and preferences. It aims to support your wellness journey by offering accessible and reliable information.
How do I get health tips from the chatbot?
To receive health tips, simply start a conversation with our chatbot and mention your specific health concerns or goals. The chatbot will analyze your input and provide tailored advice to help you maintain or improve your health.
Can the chatbot recommend food based on my dietary preferences?
Yes, the chatbot can provide food recommendations that align with your dietary preferences and nutritional needs. Whether you follow a specific diet or have certain food restrictions, the chatbot will suggest options that are suitable for you.
How does the chatbot suggest medicines?
The chatbot uses a database of medical information to suggest over-the-counter medicines and remedies for common ailments. For more serious conditions, it advises consulting a healthcare professional for a proper diagnosis and prescription.
Is the information provided by the chatbot reliable?
Our chatbot is designed to offer information based on verified medical sources and guidelines. However, it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult your healthcare provider for any health concerns.
Can the chatbot assist with mental health concerns?
Yes, the chatbot can provide general tips for mental well-being and suggest resources for managing stress, anxiety, and other common mental health issues. For more serious concerns, we recommend seeking help from a licensed mental health professional.
How do I provide feedback about the chatbot?
We value your feedback and are constantly looking to improve our services. You can provide feedback by clicking on the feedback button in the chatbot interface or by contacting our support team through the contact us page.
